All 4-some and matches are scheduled by the Golf League, so please inform the league scheduler of any planned no-shows, i.e., vacations, business trips, medical leave, as soon as you know. On play days if you find that you cannot be there please notify the members of your group and the scheduler. The goal is to have each player in a 4-some. There are 2-8 week halves to the season with a playoff between the first and second half winner. There will be other special events, such as "fun nites"-team scrambles. GOLD tees for seniors. 4-SOME & MATCH REQUESTS WILL BE ACCEPTED FOR THE FIRST 2 WEEKS OF EACH HALF. There are prizes awarded for each half, for overall play and for low-net scoring.
